# Content Rules

## Source Safety

- Formal publication requires human verification of every item.
- Prefer official announcements, government releases, company blogs, research institutions, papers, standards bodies, and reputable media.
- Do not use unverified social media rumors.
- Do not use internal, confidential, sensitive, non-public, or workplace-specific information.
- Do not exaggerate AI capabilities or imply AI replaces human judgment.
- For disputed or policy-sensitive items, use neutral wording and mark `待核验`.

## Candidate Selection

For an extended weekly video, select up to 8 items:

- 3-4 China domestic items.
- 3-4 international items.
- Include governance/safety, application/industry, and infrastructure/model capability if possible.
- Avoid making the video a list of product advertisements.

## Writing Style

Use:

- "5月26日，国家能源局在深圳召开..."
- "5月27日，OpenAI发布..."
- "据[具体来源]报道..." only when the source is media rather than official.

Avoid:

- "公开报道显示"
- "相关方面"
- "震惊", "炸裂", "颠覆", "遥遥领先"
- absolute predictions or promotional claims

## Video Copy Limits

- Title: usually <= 16 Chinese characters.
- Keywords: max 3.
- `screen_text`: one concise signal sentence.
- `fact`: concrete event sentence, ideally 1-2 lines on screen.
- `why_matters` and `work_relevance`: short, sober, non-directive.

Every item must let viewers understand the actual event, not just a category label.
